About the Club

The Programming Club at Greenwich High School seeks to engage all students, experienced and inexperienced, who have an interest in coding to learn more about the applications of computer science and gain experience with several programming languages. Our club hopes to not only teach beginners in the basics of HTML and JavaScript but engage more experienced programmers with projects and a variety of app development competitions.
